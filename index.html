Rollercosty is a thrilling rollercoaster experience that takes you on a journey through a 3D model of a PC. The rollercoaster flows seamlessly through key components of the computer, such as the RAM, GPU, CPU, and more. This unique ride combines education and excitement, offering an up-close look at the inner workings of a PC while delivering the adrenaline rush of a high-speed adventure. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or a thrill-seeker, Rollercosty is a ride like no other! The project is made using three.js.
